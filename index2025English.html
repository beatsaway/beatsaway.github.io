Artist Statement

I am a multimedia artist and interactive sound designer exploring the intersection of technology, sound, and human interaction. My artistic practice encompasses gamified art exhibitions, sound art installations, generative music applications, VR experiences, and interactive interfaces that challenge traditional boundaries between digital and physical spaces.

My work investigates how sound, gesture, and technology can create immersive experiences that engage audiences in new ways. Through projects involving micro-bit programming, finger gesture mapping, gesture instrument design, and VJ applications, I explore the relationship between human movement, sound, and visual feedback.

Drawing from over 12 years of experience in game sound design and music production, I create interactive installations that blend technical innovation with artistic expression. My practice questions how we can use technology not just as a tool, but as a medium for creating meaningful human experiences and connections.
